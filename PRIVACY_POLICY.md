## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Readian ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By using Readian, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Public Information
The following information is publicly visible on your profile:

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes

- Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

If Readian is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

### 4.5 With Your Consent
We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored on secure servers
- Images are stored on Cloudinary's secure CDN
- Database is hosted on MongoDB Atlas with encryption

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Password encryption using bcrypt
- HTTPS encryption for data transmission
- JWT tokens for secure authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Update
You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Update your profile information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Download your data

**How to exercise:** Log in to your account and visit the profile settings page.

### 6.2 Delete Account
You can request account deletion at any time:
- Contact us at privacy@readian.com
- Account and associated data will be deleted within 30 days
- Published books may be retained for legal and archival purposes

### 6.3 Opt-Out of Communications
You can opt-out of:
- Marketing emails (via unsubscribe link)
- Non-essential notifications (in account settings)
- **Note:** You cannot opt-out of essential service emails (e.g., password resets)

### 6.4 Cookie Management
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Block all cookies
- Delete existing cookies
- Allow cookies from specific sites

**Note:** Blocking cookies may limit functionality.

### 6.5 Data Portability
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
- Transfer your data to another service

**How to request:** Email privacy@readian.com

## 7. Children's Privacy

### 7.1 Age Requirements
- Minimum age to create an account: **13 years old**
- Access to adult content (18+ rated): **18 years old**

### 7.2 Parental Consent
- Users under 18 should have parental consent
- Parents can request deletion of minor's account

### 7.3 Age Verification
- Users must provide their age during profile setup
- Age determines access to age-restricted content
- False age information violates our Terms and Conditions

### 7.4 Content Filtering
- Kids content (0-17): Accessible to all users
- Adult content (18+): Restricted to verified users 18 and older

### 12.1 Rights

- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of exercising privacy rights

### 12.2 How to Exercise Your Rights
Email us at privacy@readian.com with:
- Your full name
- Email address associated with your account
